About

I am an eclectic designer who finds inspiration everywhere. My degree in Technical Arts for the theatre ultimately prepared me for the design world—tight deadlines, collaborating with clients, and project management. I am currently a full-time graphic designer for Garden & Gun magazine and love being a part of multiple collaborative projects at once. Whether it’s managing design concepts for G&G’s e-commerce site, art directing images for promotional layouts, or branding integrated marketing events, I always have a hand in new ways to share my vibrant aesthetic with the readers and followers of G&G’s award-winning lifestyle brand.

Lets work together!
